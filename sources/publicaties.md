# Publications IKNL

## What is this source?
All official IKNL publications can be found on the IKNL website: https://iknl.nl/onderzoek/publicaties

The Netherlands Comprehensive Cancer Organisation (IKNL) generates high‑quality scientific insights based on data from the Netherlands Cancer Registry (NKR). These insights are published as peer‑reviewed scientific articles and reports covering:

- Trends and developments in cancer care in the Netherlands
- Societal issues related to cancer
- Outcomes, diagnostics, and treatments for specific cancer types

Together, these publications form an important evidence base for researchers, clinicians, policymakers, and the wider oncology community.
<br><br>

## Scientific Publications
IKNL has many scientific publications, but its difficult to navigate them.
- They are listed under “Overzicht wetenschappelijke publicaties”
- Articles span many years, cancer types, and research themes
- Not all publications are open access
- Metadata and filtering options for bulk exploration or analysis are non-existing
